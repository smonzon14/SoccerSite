How to Sign Up:
  1. Have your latest physical on hand from your doctor.
  2. Create a student ID on Family ID here.
  3. Fill out any information on Family ID and sign up for Soccer on the site.
  4. Make a check out to Melrose High School with the correct amount determined for you based on your financial situation.
